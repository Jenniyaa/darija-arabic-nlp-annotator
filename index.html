How it works
01
Paste your raw text
Arabic, Darija, French, or mixed — single entry or batch. Franco-Arab and code-switched text are fully supported.
02
Choose your settings
Select annotation level (Basic or Detailed) and export format. Basic returns sentiment and language. Detailed adds intent, topics, flags, and notes.
03
Copy or export
Download your structured output as JSON or CSV — ready for Label Studio, UHRS, Scale AI, or Supabase.
